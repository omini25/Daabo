What device insurance covers

A stand-alone device insurance policy through Daabo includes coverage for perils (laptop theft, water damage, fire damage, and more) and unexpected accidents such as:

Water damage (including liquid spills and submersion)

Screen damage from from dropping or stepping on your laptop

Accidental damage of device

Accidental loss

International cover so you are protected when you travel

Upgrade assistance for when you want to change the device

What device insurance doesn't cover for laptops and computers

Device insurance doesn't cover everything. Some exclusions are:

Computer viruses

Wear and tear or corrosion

Software issues

Blatant neglect

Why choose Daabo Device Insurance to protect your computers and laptops?

Device insurance through Daabo protects you from theft and various types of accidental damage, including liquid spills and cracked screens. New computers, laptops and used or used models can also be covered. You can purchase computer or laptop coverage at any time.
